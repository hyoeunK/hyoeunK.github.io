Synopsis Sung-Chil, an ill-tempered, stubborn old men working at the supermarket, meets Geum-Nim, a flower shop owner. After meeting each other, Sung-Chil fall in love with her and begins to show changes for the better. However, numerous challenges await on the long road of love and happiness.

  • Managed all aspects of the Artwork and Trailer
  • Wrote a media kit
  • Setting up film promotional events

Marketing Overview

‘Salute D’Amour’ is a warm, heartfelt movie that tells the story of a romantic relationship between an elderly couple. However, this film didn’t have easy marketability. The love story of an elderly couple was not interesting subject to public and it was hard to explain the jist of the story to the audiences without giving way too much of the story because the movie has a twist. Although audiences’ satisfaction was high after watching this film, they had very low preference to it. To overcome these problems, we held large scale preview screening to make positive buzz for this film. As a result, the film reached over 1 million views beyond our expectation.

Marketing Keywords

  • Warm and heartfelt movie
  • Veteran actor's acting
  • Best movie for parents and family
