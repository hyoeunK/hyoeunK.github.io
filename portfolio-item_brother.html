My Annoying Brother

Synopsis Doo-Sik gets paroled from prison thanks to his younger brother Doo-Young who was a promising judo athlete but got involved in an accident before the selection for the national team. Doo-Sik suddenly appears in front of Doo-Young and they begin to live together.

  • Developed marketing and promotional strategies
  • Managed all aspects of the Artwork and Trailer
  • Executed Social media marketing campaign
  • Wrote a media kit
  • Setting up film promotional events

Marketing Overview

‘My annoying Brother’ is a comedy drama film dealing with family relationship between two different brothers but the subject matter of this movie could be seen as negative and serious to the audience because one of the main character suddenly became lose his sight and another had criminal record. To avoid negative impression of this movie, we focused on comedy genre and humanity of the characters. Also, as two main actors was popular among young women, online marketing showing funny and casual relationship between them was our priority plan to attract audiences’ attention. As a result, the film topped local box office and hit more than one million views within four days of its release. The film reached 3 million views in mid December.

Marketing Keywords

  • Comic buddy movie
  • Chemistry between main actors
  • 2030 women target online marketing
