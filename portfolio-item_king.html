Marketing Overview

‘The King's Case Note’ is comedy historical drama dealing with traditional science investigation. This film is an adaptation of famous comic books but character’s setting is changed a lot, especially age and appearance, so many original works’ fans was disappointed. Also, at the beginning of the marketing plan, it was hard to attract audience’s attention because the actors were not very popular at that time. We decided to hide the relationship with original comic books as much as possible and focused on interesting settings of the film such as traditional science investigation and adventure. As a result, this film reached about 1 million views.

Marketing Keywords

  • Traditional Scientific investigation
  • Intersting character setting
  • Chemistry of Comic duo
