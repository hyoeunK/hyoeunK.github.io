Game Design

Pet Character
‘Chieftain Jr.’ is the first interactive pet in the game. Chieftain is the most popular monster in the game, so we made baby version of the monster as user’s pet.
Role
Designed character's skills, actions, and combat styles. Especially I suggested interactive skills of this pet at first. The pet can dance with player to increase personal intimacy and express its emotions such as anger, hunger, and pleasure. Depending on its mood, the ability of the pet varies.

Season 2 - Sub Stories
Season 2 was the biggest update after releasing the game because it introduced different part of the game world (Ocean and Dessert) and started whole new story with new characters.
Role
Wrote 22 episodes in Season 2 about new adventures and NPCs backstory.
