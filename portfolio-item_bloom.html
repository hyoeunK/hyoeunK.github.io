Bloomengine Brand Marketing

Video Script Writing, Jul 2018 - Aug 2018

Overview I worked with the production team ‘Creative silver’ which is a team of multimedia artists in New York as a part of ‘Bloomengine’ promotion campaign. This campaign was for introducing products to potential investors and customers to achieve desirable fundraising. The team of Bloomengine already had a commercial video that focused on the technology of the product, but they needed a new promotional video to expand its customer target. So, I was responsible for inventing concepts and copy that can attract new customers by touching more emotional part. In addition, I worked with the main director to write the video script for shooting. I suggested that ‘Children’ and ‘Education’ would be effective marketing keyword to appeal customers who have children. In order to emphasize that it is a great help to educate children to learn how to grow plants, I suggested the scenes that children and parents look and draw pictures of the plants in Bloomengine together.

My Responsibilities

  • Wrote 3 minutes video scripts geared toward promoting kickstart funding campaign
  • Helped coordinate and manage a 1-day shoot in New York as an assistant producer
  • Worked closely with a video production team
