Like for Likes

Synopsis A notorious TV drama writer and a Korean wave star who fight like cats and dogs every time they meet; a spinster flight attendant landlady and a nosy chef tenant who build a sense of camaraderie; a genius relationship-virgin composer who harbors a secret admiration for a scatterbrained producer. As they crash-land into each other’s lives, will there be love?

Responsibilities

  • Developed marketing and promotional strategies
  • Managed all aspects of the Artwork and Trailer
  • Developed engaging, vital content to build social media audience
  • Setting up film promotional events

Marketing Overview

‘Like for Likes’ features ensemble cast of well-known actors in South Korea and shows different aspects of love through 4 separate stories involving a variety of individuals. We focused on different generation’s way of love and appealed it effectively to separated targets. Also, we selected SNS influencers as film supporters and sent them special film merchandises for online PR. Although this film got a lot of attention, it reached only 800 thousands views.
