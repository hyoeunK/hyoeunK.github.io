Seondal: The Man Who Sells the River

Synopsis Based on an ancient novel, Seondal is a genius swindler, possessing an alluring appearance and boldness. His swindler team is famous nationwide and decide to steal tobacco which is the most expensive traded product.

  • Developed engaging, vital content to build social media audience
  • Wrote a media kit
  • Setting up film promotional events
  • Collated and distributed event materials

Marketing Overview

‘Seondal: The Man Who Sells the River’ is trendy Heist film with young and fancy casting based on famous ancient novel in South Korea. The background of this film was very widely known to all generations but main actor was too young to appeal 3040 people so we emphasized teamwork of actors consisting of various ages to establish this film as family movie. Although the opening score of this film was high, unfortunately this film didn’t live up to the expectations of the audiences. As a result, The film reached about 2 million views.
