The Admiral:Roaring Currents

Synopsis In the year 1597, when the Joseon Dynasty faces imminent ruin by the Japanese, disgraced Admiral YI Sun-shin is summoned as a last resort to lead the kingdom’s remaining 12 ships and an army of doubtful soldiers against the Emperor’s 330 strong fleet.

Marketing Overview

Based on the historical Battle of Myeongnyang, it stars an ensemble cast and crew. Also, the highest production cost in South Korea and the most realistic naval battle scenes were major features of this film. At that time, as people were impressed by the hidden historical facts and the leadership of the Admiral Yi, this film was regarded as must-watch film. Every politicians and CEOs supported this film to learn Admiral’s leadership and general public wanted to learn history through this film. As the film recorded 10 million admissions only 12 days after its premiere, it set a record for achieving such a high number of viewers in the shortest amount of time. The movie also surpassed Avatar’s record of 13 million viewers to become the most-watched and highest-grossing film of all time in South Korea, with 17.6 million admissions and a worldwide gross of US$135.9 million.
