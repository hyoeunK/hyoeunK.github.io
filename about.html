About

Hyoeun Kim

I am a Master’s student at Carnegie Mellon University majoring Entertainment Technology. I am passionate about interactive experience design in VR/AR field in order to connect people and technology in a creative way through new media.

I was born in South Korea and double majored in Korean Literature and Business Administration at EWHA Womans University. Before I moved in the U.S., I worked as a Film marketing senior analyst at CJ Entertainment, South Korea’s leading film production studio, for four years, and participated in over sixteen film marketing projects. Also, I have professional experience in game industry. I worked as a game designer of ‘Vindictus’ for a year at Nexon, the biggest online game company in South Korea, and created game stories and characters. Combining my knowledge and professional experiences, I aim to pursue a career as an experience designer of interactive media.
