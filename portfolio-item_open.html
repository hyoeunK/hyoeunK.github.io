Co-interviewer and article editor, Sep 2018 - Now

Overview ‘Opencreators’ is a weekly updated online interview magazine for Korean and we interview with inspiring people who try to create new things or who try to challenge themselves to achieve their own goal. We focus on finding lessons and new ideas from people’s story and we want to share their story with other people. I am responsible for interviewing people with Paul who is a project manager and I edit the article. Until now, I have interviewed over 10 people and wrote articles and, currently, we are preparing to support various language including English to spread our interview articles.
