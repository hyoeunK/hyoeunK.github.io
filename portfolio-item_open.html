Opencreators

Co-interviewer and article editor, Sep 2018 - Now

Overview ‘Opencreators’ is a weekly updated online interview magazine for Koreans in which we interview inspiring people who try to create new things or who try to challenge themselves to achieve their own goals. We focus on finding lessons and new ideas from people’s stories and share their stories with other people. I am responsible for interviewing people with Junhwan Paul Kang who is a project manager, and I edit the articles. Until now, I have interviewed over 10 people and wrote articles and, currently, we are preparing to support various languages including English to expand our audience.

My Responsibilities

  • Interviewed with people and wrote the articles
  • Suggested an interviewee list
