Baby Adventure

Producer and 3D artist, Sep 2019

Overview 'Baby Adventure' is a VR adventure game for HTC Vive. A guest becomes a baby in virtual world, explores the virtual world and interact with objects freely. The goal of this project is giving a freedom to the guest.

My Responsibilities

  • Designing and prototyping the level to accommodate the player's field of view in AR
  • 3D modeliing and texturing assets using Maya, photoshop, and substance painter
  • leading the development process of story writing, concept design, level design

  • Project: Game development
  • Development time: 2 weeks
  • Game Engine Unity
